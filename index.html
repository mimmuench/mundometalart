Questions

Frequently asked by realtors

Can I see the design before I commit?
Yes. Every project begins with a free mockup. Once you’re happy with the layout and typography, we’ll finalise production-ready files for your fabricator.
Do you also help with size & placement?
Absolutely. Send a quick photo of the entrance, column or wall and we’ll recommend ideal sizes and proportions so the sign feels integrated with the architecture.
Can you match my agency branding?
As an architect and designer, we can echo your existing identity – typography, logo or subtle brand marks – while keeping the piece timeless and homeowner-focused.
What file types will I receive?
You’ll receive clean vector files (typically DXF/SVG/PDF) ready for laser cutting, plus a high-resolution mockup image you can share with your client.
